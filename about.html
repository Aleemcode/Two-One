About Us

What We Do?

Two-One Venture supplies Aggregates based on specification mostly across the South-West states of Nigeria, though, our clients in the South West have seen reasons to take us outside the region to as far as the South-South states (particularly Calabar/Port-Harcourt) whenever they have construction projects in the area.

Any construction, from a small building, roads, even skyscrapers requires high-quality raw materials in large quantities. We cannot deny the importance of a strong foundation when it comes to the construction business. Good quality aggregate material is undoubtedly the fundamental building block 'literally' of any construction.
